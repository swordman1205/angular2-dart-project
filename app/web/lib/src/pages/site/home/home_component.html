Empowering Women, Period.

The product is simple to use and empowers women to effectively keep control of their health.

We have build a unique menstrual pad, which can be used for wellness monitoring and diagnostics. It looks, feels like and is used like any normal menstrual pad but also provides health insights. The pads will eventually be able to utilize menstrual blood to detect biomarkers, vitamins, minerals, and disease markers.

FAQ

Q: Is it harmful to participate in the study?
A: The pad is organic, cotton based and has no added chemicals. We have tested the product on +500 women with no issue. A professional phlebotomist will perform a blood draw from where you will feel a small pinch on your arm, which may create some discomfort.
Q: Will I get compensated to participate?
A: To participate all you need to do is use the pads and schedule a time for a professional phlebotomist to come to your house and do a quick blood draw. We will compensate you with $25 for your time.
Q: Can I still participate in the testing if I live outside the San Diego or Palo Alto area?
A: For this product you have to be in the San Diego or San Francisco/Palo Alto area. Please sign up for our newsletter to know when it is possible to try the product in your city.
Q: Will I receive the results from the blood testing?
A: Currently we are unable to provide you with the values unless any biomarkers are abnormal in which case our medical staff will contact you.
Q: Who will see my data and information?
A: All samples are de-identified. Only study staff will be able look up samples if values are abnormally high, in which case our medical staff will contact you.
Q: How do you protect my data?
A: All data from this study is encrypted and stored securely. The data will not be shared with 3rd parties outside research staff. Please see more in our Privacy Policy and reach out to us if you have questions.
Q: How much time does it take to participate?
A: It takes about an hour to complete the study excluding the time you use the Q-pads.
Q: Where will I get the blood draw?
A: A professional nurse will call you and coordinate with you where and when a good place to take the blood draw is. Ideally, the nurse will meet at your home.
